About The Event

CodeVerse is a team-based technical competition designed to foster problem-solving, creativity, and collaboration among students from our department and other colleges. The event will consist of multiple rounds testing participants' skills in coding proficiency, algorithm design, critical thinking, and system architecture.

Teams of two will compete across four exciting rounds, with prizes for the top performers and participation certificates for all attendees.
