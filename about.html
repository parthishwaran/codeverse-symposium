About CodeVerse

Learn more about our inter-college technical symposium

Our Mission

The CodeVerse Symposium is designed to foster problem-solving, creativity, and collaboration among students from our department and other colleges. We aim to provide a platform where participants can showcase their technical skills while learning from their peers.

Event Highlights

Team Competition

Participate in teams of 2 members to solve challenging problems together

4 Technical Rounds

Test your skills in coding, algorithms, system design, and technical knowledge

Exciting Prizes

Win mementos, certificates, and recognition for your skills

Networking

Connect with fellow CS/IT students from multiple colleges

Who Can Participate?

  • Undergraduate and postgraduate students
  • Teams of 2 members from the same institution
  • Students from CS, DS, AI/ML domains
  • Open to participants from other colleges
